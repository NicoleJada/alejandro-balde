Alejandro Balde

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alejandro Balde Martínez is a Spanish professional footballer who plays as a left-back for La Liga club FC Barcelona and the Spain national team[1]. He is known for his pace and attacking runs down the left flank.

1. Early life

Balde was born on 18 October 2003 in Barcelona, Catalonia[2]. His father is from Guinea-Bissau and his mother is from the Dominican Republic. He joined FC Barcelona’s La Masia academy at age 8.

2. Club career

Balde made his first team debut for Barcelona in 2021[3]. He became the starting left-back in the 2022-2023 season after Jordi Alba’s departure.

  • 2021: Debut vs Club Brugge in Champions League
  • 2023: Won La Liga title with Barcelona
  • 2024: Extended contract until 2028

3. International career

Balde debuted for Spain in 2022[4]. He was selected for the 2022 FIFA World Cup in Qatar at age 19.

  • Spain U16, U17, U19 youth teams
  • Senior debut: 2022 vs Jordan
  • 2022 World Cup squad

4. Style of play

Balde is recognized for his speed, dribbling, and ability to support attacks[5]. He often plays as an attacking full-back, similar to Dani Alves.
